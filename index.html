Learn About AI

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines. It involves the use of algorithms to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence.

Types of AI: There are two main types of AI: Narrow AI (Weak AI) and General AI (Strong AI). Narrow AI is designed to perform a specific task, while General AI has the ability to understand, learn, and apply knowledge across various domains.

AI Technologies: Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Computer Vision are some of the key technologies driving advancements in AI.

Explore AI Applications

Industry Applications: AI is revolutionizing industries such as healthcare (diagnosis and personalized medicine), finance (fraud detection and algorithmic trading), and education (personalized learning).

Everyday AI: Experience AI in your daily life through virtual assistants like Siri and Alexa, recommendation systems on streaming platforms, and smart home devices that adapt to your preferences.
